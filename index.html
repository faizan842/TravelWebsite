    Live a unique experience in Qatar


    Sophisticated, modern, traditional, luxurious: Qatar checks every box. Go on, select your guide and soak up all that Qatar has to offer.

    A public art itinerary

    Want to spend the day experiencing the best public art across Doha? After getting a taste of the art scene through the many pieces at Hamad International Airport, indulge by venturing across the city, to see the public art pieces scattered across it.

    Active itinerary

    With its arid beauty, sunny climes and untrammeled deserts, Qatar has long called to adventurers. Answering that call has never been more rewarding. Hit the water, bash the dunes, surf the skies, fish the seas and ride sleek Arabian horses.

    Cultural itinerary

    Begin your cultural tour of Qatar by planning well – because there is plenty to experience. From traditional souqs, to museums, art galleries and installations to UNESCO heritage sites and forts outside it, culture, art, and history are everywhere in Qatar.

    Business traveller

    Qatar is a financial, trading and business centre of global significance – and a world-class hub for culture, relaxation, adventure, history, and sport.
